The nine elements

Most tantric writing names four or five practices and stops. The actual working vocabulary is broader:

  1. Breath — the only autonomic system you voluntarily control
  2. Sound — vocalization, vagus-nerve modulation
  3. Voice and singing — sustained tone from the lower body
  4. Dance — full-body unscripted movement from impulse
  5. Movement — slow deliberate, somatic shaking, neurogenic tremor
  6. Touch — self, partnered, comfort, erotic
  7. Chant — rhythmic vocalization with intention
  8. Focused attention — sustained without leaking
  9. Energy work — the most distinctive element. Not metaphysics — the felt, generated, moveable sensation in an aroused body

Each of these will get its own dedicated article. The full integration is in the forthcoming book.
