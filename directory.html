What's coming

A searchable directory of practitioners across the body-and-sexuality landscape:

  • Tantric teachers and body-workers — across in-person and online practice
  • Tantric massage practitioners — yoni / lingam / full-body, vetted by track record and consent practice
  • Sex workers — independents, especially those operating with skill, presence, and clean energetics. The world's oldest practitioners of body-knowledge, given the visibility their work has earned and the moralizing culture has denied them.
  • Ritualists — practitioners running ritual containers (death-rites, sex-rites, fire ceremony, etc.)
  • Photographers working at the intersection of body and image — not generic "boudoir" but practitioners using the lens as part of the work
  • Hypnotists / erotic-hypnotists with documented practice

The transparency standard

Every listing in this directory includes, at the top, before any other information:

  1. Their direct teachers — by name, with dates of training
  2. Their lineage — what tradition they were trained in (Osho, Margot Anand, Daka Daniel Odier, Sky Dancing, Source School of Tantra, Quodoushka, etc.)
  3. Any cult associations — past or current. Disclosed honestly. The reader gets to make an informed choice. The teacher who came up through Bhagwan / Rajneesh in the 80s says so. The teacher who studied with David Deida lists it. The teacher who did Landmark says it.
  4. Their actual practice — what they offer, prices, where they work, contact

This is the opposite of how the modern tantric directory ecosystem currently works (vague spiritual-titles, no traceable lineage, no cult disclosure). The reader has been operating blind for decades. We are restoring sight.
